Hack Themes

Agriculture

Increasing agricultural productivity, developing sustainable food and supply chains, and tackling soil erosion and irrigation issues are some of the challenges we face. Got an idea to resolve these problems?

Smart infrastructure

Improved infrastructure in cities and homes is a feasible solution to solve the issues of quality living and sustainability in densely populated cities. Showcase your ideas on this cool topic now!

Healthcare

In the Healthcare track, we aim to develop technology that will provide solutions to health-related issues, including the COVID-19 pandemic, mental health, etc.

Sustainable Development

Creating solutions that are climate friendly and relate to the Earth, while meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Fintech

Technology that seeks to improve and automate the delivery and use of financial services. It is intended to help businesses, business owners, and consumers better manage their financial operations and processes.

Open Innovation

Interested in an idea that doesn't fit our track themes? Use this theme to display and present your ideas. Create something that bridges these tracks or something that is completely new.
