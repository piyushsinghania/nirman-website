Hack Themes

Agriculture

Improving agricultural productivity, ensuring sustainable food security, not just for this generation but for generations to come, is a very important challenge. Got an idea to solve the big problems? Welcome, you belong here!

Smart city

With fast growing technologies, the cities are rapidly becoming smart. So, now is the best time to showcase your ideas on this cool topic.

Healthcare

The Healthcare track aims to create innovative technology that will help provide solutions for health-related issues. This includes modern public health challenges like COVID-19 pandemic, mental health etc.

Mining

With the growth of technology the requirement of natural resources are increasing day by day. And the mining industry are in boom to cater such high demands. Showcase your ideas to tackle and solve the problems in the mining industry.

FinTech

It is the new tech that seeks to improve and automate the delivery and use of financial services. It is utilized to help companies, business owners and consumers better manage their financial operations and processes.

Open theme

If your idea doesn't match our track, then you are free to display and work on your ideas under the open innovation theme.
