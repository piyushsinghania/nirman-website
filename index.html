Hack Themes

Agriculture

Improving agricultural productivity, ensuring sustainable food security, not just for this generation but for generations to come, is a very important challenge. Got an idea to solve the big problems? Welcome, you belong here!

Smart infrastructure

With highly populated cities, better infrastructure in cities and homes can help in resolving limitations and issues. Build something that solves the issues of quality living with sustainability. So, now is the best time to showcase your ideas on this cool topic.

Healthcare

The Healthcare track aims to create innovative technology that will help provide solutions for health-related issues. This includes modern public health challenges like COVID-19 pandemic, mental health etc.

Sustainable Development

Development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It aims to create solutions that are climate positive actions and are related to earth.

Fintech

It is the new tech that seeks to improve and automate the delivery and use of financial services. It is utilized to help companies, business owners and consumers better manage their financial operations and processes.

Open Innovation

Idea doesn't match our track themes? then you are free to display and work on your ideas under this theme. Build something that is bridge between these tracks or something that is absolutely novel.
